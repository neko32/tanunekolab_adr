# ADR Doc Status

Proposed ... The Proposal was made but not yet acknowledged to start the discussion.
WIP ... The Proposal is still in discussion.
Accepted ... All stakeholders approved the decision. A final status unless superceded or rescinded.
Rejected ... All stakeholders did not approve the decision. See Decision section for reasons for the rejection. A final status.
Cancelled ... The proposal was rescended by the proponent. A final status.
Rescinded ... Accepted once but cancelled later. See decision section why it was rescinded. A final status.
Superceded ... Final Status. Amended by another ADR.
