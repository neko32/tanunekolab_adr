# 11. l4 ticket

Date: 2026-04-28

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Any L4 level ticket must contain defined data

## Decision

We adopt a standardized L4 ticket template across all supported ticketing systems. This ensures consistent capture of essential information, clear prioritization, and alignment with our internal review and escalation practices.

- GitHub (Ja): [template](../l4_ticket_template/github_issues_ja.md)
- GitHub (En): TBD

## Consequences

### Positive

- Ticket quality becomes more predictable, supporting faster triage and clearer handoffs.
- Stakeholders gain confidence that L4 issues include key details such as impact, reproducibility, and mitigation options.
- Cross-functional teams can more effectively collaborate on high-severity work with a unified information structure.

### Negative

- Creating L4 tickets requires more effort from authors, particularly up front when documenting context and requirements.
- Reviewers and PMs may need to enforce the template until it becomes established team practice.
- Incomplete or improperly formatted tickets may be rejected or delayed to preserve consistency.
